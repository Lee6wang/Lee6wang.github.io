# Happy New Year: To Every Developer Still on the Road

First of all, I want to say to everyone: Happy New Year!

For many developers, 2025 was not an easy year.
Driven by AI, the pace kept getting faster, changes became denser, and the pressure from life and work pressed more directly into every day.

Looking at my own development state, I often felt as if I was "running while eating."
I was moving forward while thinking about where the future should go.
Sometimes I seemed to figure something out late at night, only to wake up the next morning and fall back into confusion.
I do not think this feeling belongs only to me.

Because of that, when we stumble and push our way to this moment in 2026, this point in time feels especially precious.
We are all moving forward through uncertainty, and within that uncertainty, we are slowly growing our own sense of certainty.

For me, one important thing over the past year was building this public account.
It allowed us to meet here, and it gave us a space where we can discuss technology in a relatively pure way.

The world moves very fast, fast enough that people can easily be swept along by it.
I hope this place can become a small "buffer zone" -
a place where I, and everyone else, can occasionally slow down and seriously think:
why did we set out, where are we going, and what is truly worth investing in over the long term?

Perhaps we only meet briefly at a certain point in time,
but I sincerely hope that pure knowledge and honest thinking can accompany you through a long part of your development journey.

For all of us in 2026, I hope:
no matter what wind and rain may come, we can still keep a pure heart;
no matter how fast things change, we can still hold to long-term thinking, keep improving, and keep growing.

Happy New Year.
May both you and I move forward firmly on the road we love.
